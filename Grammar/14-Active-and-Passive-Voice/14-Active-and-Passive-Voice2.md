# 📚 Remaining Tense-wise Conversion Rules

In **Part 1**, we learned:

* ✅ Simple Present
* ✅ Present Continuous
* ✅ Present Perfect

Now let's complete the remaining tenses.

---

# 4️⃣ Simple Past Tense

## 🟢 Active Structure

```text
Subject + V2 + Object
```

## 🔵 Passive Structure

```text
Object + was/were + V3 + by + Subject
```

### Examples

✅ Active: Rahul wrote a letter.

✅ Passive: A letter was written by Rahul.

---

✅ Active: They cleaned the classroom.

✅ Passive: The classroom was cleaned by them.

---

# 5️⃣ Past Continuous Tense

## 🟢 Active Structure

```text
Subject + was/were + V-ing + Object
```

## 🔵 Passive Structure

```text
Object + was/were + being + V3
```

### Examples

✅ Active: She was preparing dinner.

✅ Passive: Dinner was being prepared by her.

---

# 6️⃣ Past Perfect Tense

## 🟢 Active Structure

```text
Subject + had + V3 + Object
```

## 🔵 Passive Structure

```text
Object + had been + V3
```

### Examples

✅ Active: They had completed the project.

✅ Passive: The project had been completed by them.

---

# 7️⃣ Simple Future Tense

## 🟢 Active Structure

```text
Subject + will + V1 + Object
```

## 🔵 Passive Structure

```text
Object + will be + V3
```

### Examples

✅ Active: She will finish the report.

✅ Passive: The report will be finished by her.

---

# 8️⃣ Future Perfect Tense

## 🟢 Active Structure

```text
Subject + will have + V3 + Object
```

## 🔵 Passive Structure

```text
Object + will have been + V3
```

### Examples

✅ Active: They will have completed the work.

✅ Passive: The work will have been completed by them.

---

# ❓ Passive Voice of Interrogative Sentences

Questions can also be changed into passive voice.

---

## Rule 1: Yes/No Questions

### Active

Do they play cricket?

### Passive

Is cricket played by them?

---

### Active

Did Rahul write the letter?

### Passive

Was the letter written by Rahul?

---

## Rule 2: WH-Questions

### Active

Who wrote this poem?

### Passive

By whom was this poem written?

---

### Active

When did she complete the work?

### Passive

When was the work completed by her?

---

# 🚫 Passive Voice of Negative Sentences

Keep **not** after the helping verb.

---

### Active

She does not cook food.

### Passive

Food is not cooked by her.

---

### Active

They did not finish the task.

### Passive

The task was not finished by them.

---

# 📝 Imperative Sentences

Imperative sentences begin with a command or request.

---

## Rule

Use:

```text
Let + Object + be + V3
```

---

### Examples

✅ Active: Open the door.

✅ Passive: Let the door be opened.

---

✅ Active: Finish the assignment.

✅ Passive: Let the assignment be finished.

---

### Requests

✅ Active: Please help me.

✅ Passive: You are requested to help me.

---

### Advice

✅ Active: Follow the rules.

✅ Passive: Let the rules be followed.

---

# ⚠️ Verbs That Cannot Be Changed into Passive Voice

Some verbs do **not** have an object, so they cannot be converted.

### Examples

❌ He sleeps.

❌ Birds fly.

❌ The baby cried.

❌ She laughed.

# 📋 Tense Summary Table

| Active Tense       | Passive Structure      |
| ------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Simple Present     | is/am/are + V3         |
| Present Continuous | is/am/are + being + V3 |
| Present Perfect    | has/have been + V3     |
| Simple Past        | was/were + V3          |
| Past Continuous    | was/were being + V3    |
| Past Perfect       | had been + V3          |
| Simple Future      | will be + V3           |
| Future Perfect     | will have been + V3    |

# ⚠️ Common Errors

### Error 1

❌ The work is complete by Rahul.

✅ The work is completed by Rahul.

---

### Error 2

❌ The project has completed.

✅ The project has been completed.

---

### Error 3

❌ The room cleaned by her.

✅ The room was cleaned by her.

---

### Error 4

❌ Homework was finish.

✅ Homework was finished.

---

### Error 5

❌ Food cooked by mother.

✅ Food was cooked by mother.
