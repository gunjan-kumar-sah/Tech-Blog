# 📝 Practice Questions

Test your understanding of **Active & Passive Voice**.

---

## Question 1

Choose the correct passive voice:

**Rahul writes a letter.**

A. A letter wrote by Rahul.

B. A letter is written by Rahul.

C. A letter was written Rahul.

D. A letter has written by Rahul.

✅ **Answer:** **B. A letter is written by Rahul.**

---

## Question 2

Choose the passive voice:

**She cleaned the room.**

A. The room cleaned by her.

B. The room is cleaned by her.

C. The room was cleaned by her.

D. The room has cleaned by her.

✅ **Answer:** **C. The room was cleaned by her.**

---

## Question 3

Which sentence is in **Active Voice**?

A. The homework was completed by the students.

B. The project has been finished.

C. The students completed the homework.

D. The report will be prepared.

✅ **Answer:** **C. The students completed the homework.**

---

## Question 4

Choose the correct passive voice:

**They will finish the project.**

A. The project will finish.

B. The project will be finished by them.

C. The project is finished.

D. The project was finished.

✅ **Answer:** **B. The project will be finished by them.**

---

## Question 5

Which verb form is used in passive voice?

A. V1

B. V2

C. V3 (Past Participle)

D. V-ing

✅ **Answer:** **C. V3 (Past Participle)**

## Question 6

Choose the correct passive sentence.

B. Food was cooked by mother.

C. Food is cook by mother.

D. Food has cook.

✅ **Answer:** **B. Food was cooked by mother.**

Which sentence **cannot** be changed into passive voice?

✅ **Answer:** **C. Birds fly.**

Choose the correct passive voice.

# 💼 Active & Passive Voice in Professional Communication

Passive voice is commonly used in reports, notices, and formal communication.

---

## Resume Example

✅ **Active**

Developed a Portfolio Website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

✅ **Passive**

A Portfolio Website was developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

> **Tip:** Active voice is generally stronger and more impactful in resumes.

---

## Interview Example

**Q:** Who completed this project?

✅ **Active:** I completed this project independently.

✅ **Passive:** The project was completed independently by me.

---

## Workplace Example

**Active**

The team completed the report yesterday.

**Passive**

The report was completed by the team yesterday.

---

## Email Example

**Active**

We have approved your application.

**Passive**

Your application has been approved.

# 📄 Active & Passive Voice Cheat Sheet

```text id="voicefinal"

ACTIVE VOICE

Subject + Verb + Object


PASSIVE VOICE

Object + Helping Verb + V3 + (by + Subject)


Examples

Write → Written

Eat → Eaten

Take → Taken

Make → Made

Go → Gone


Passive Structures

Simple Present
→ is/am/are + V3

Simple Past
→ was/were + V3

Simple Future
→ will be + V3

Present Perfect
→ has/have been + V3

Past Perfect
→ had been + V3

Present Continuous
→ is/am/are + being + V3

Past Continuous
→ was/were + being + V3

Future Perfect
→ will have been + V3

Imperative
→ Let + Object + be + V3

```
