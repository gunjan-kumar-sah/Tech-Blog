# 📝 Adverbs Practice Questions

Test your understanding of **Adverbs** with these questions.

---

## Question 1

He speaks ______.

A. fluent

B. fluently

C. fluency

D. more fluent

✅ **Answer:** **B. fluently**

**Explanation:** "Speaks" is a verb, so we need an **adverb**.

---

## Question 2

She completed the assignment ______.

A. careful

B. carefully

C. care

D. caring

✅ **Answer:** **B. carefully**

---

## Question 3

I will call you ______.

A. tomorrow

B. beautiful

C. honest

D. quick

✅ **Answer:** **A. tomorrow**

---

## Question 4

He is ______ late for class.

A. always

B. beautiful

C. smart

D. large

✅ **Answer:** **A. always**

---

## Question 5

She is ______ intelligent.

A. very

B. careful

C. quick

D. many

✅ **Answer:** **A. very**

---

## Question 6

They looked ______ for the missing keys.

A. everywhere

B. beautiful

C. honest

D. slow

✅ **Answer:** **A. everywhere**

---

## Question 7

He runs ______ than his brother.

A. faster

B. fast

C. fastest

D. more fast

✅ **Answer:** **A. faster**

---

## Question 8

She solved the puzzle ______.

A. easy

B. easily

C. easiest

D. easier

✅ **Answer:** **B. easily**

---

## Question 9

The train arrived ______.

A. late

B. lately

C. latest

D. laterest

✅ **Answer:** **A. late**

**Note:** *Late* itself is an adverb in this context.

---

## Question 10

He did the work ______.

A. good

B. well

C. better

D. best

✅ **Answer:** **B. well**

# 💼 Adverbs in Professional Communication

Using adverbs correctly makes your English sound natural and professional.

---

## Resume Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I completed projects successful.

✅ Correct:

> I completed projects **successfully**.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I speak English fluent.

✅ Correct:

> I speak English **fluently**.

---

## Workplace Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I work careful.

✅ Correct:

> I work **carefully**.

---

## Email Example

❌ Incorrect:

> Please reply quick.

✅ Correct:

> Please reply **quickly**.

# ⚠️ Common Errors

## Error 1

❌ She dances graceful.

✅ She dances **gracefully**.

---

## Error 2

❌ He finished the work slow.

✅ He finished the work **slowly**.

---

## Error 3

❌ I feel happily today.

✅ I feel **happy** today.

**Reason:** After linking verbs (feel, look, seem, become), use an **adjective**, not an adverb.

---

## Error 4

❌ He is real intelligent.

✅ He is **really** intelligent.

---

## Error 5

❌ She writes more faster.

✅ She writes **faster**.
