# 📖 What is an Adverb?

An **Adverb** is a word that modifies or describes:

* A Verb
* An Adjective
* Another Adverb

In simple words:

> **An adverb tells us how, when, where, or how much something happens.**

---

# 🌍 Examples

## Example 1

> He runs **quickly**.

Quickly describes the verb "runs".

---

## Example 2

> She is **very** beautiful.

Very describes the adjective "beautiful".

---

## Example 3

> He speaks **quite** slowly.

Quite describes another adverb "slowly".

---

# 🎯 Why Are Adverbs Important?

Adverbs help us:

* Make sentences clearer
* Explain actions better
* Improve speaking skills
* Write professional content
* Express emotions

---

# 🔍 How to Identify an Adverb?

Ask these questions:

---

## 1. How?

Example:

> He completed the work quickly.

How did he complete?

Answer:

Quickly → Adverb

---

## 2. When?

Example:

> I will call you tomorrow.

When will I call?

Answer:

Tomorrow → Adverb

---

## 3. Where?

Example:

> Come here.

Where?

Answer:

Here → Adverb

---

## 4. How Often?

Example:

> She always helps me.

How often?

Answer:

Always → Adverb

# 1️⃣ Adverb of Manner

## 📖 Definition

Adverbs of manner describe **how an action happens**.

They usually end with:

```
-ly
```

---

# Common Examples

* Quickly
* Slowly
* Carefully
* Easily
* Happily
* Loudly
* Quietly

---

# Examples

✅ He drives carefully.

✅ She sings beautifully.

✅ They worked honestly.

---

# Formation of Adverb of Manner

Usually:

```text id="adv01"

Adjective + ly = Adverb

```

---

Examples:

| Adjective | Adverb      |
| --------- | ----------- |
| Quick     | Quickly     |
| Slow      | Slowly      |
| Happy     | Happily     |
| Careful   | Carefully   |
| Beautiful | Beautifully |

---

# ⚠️ Important Exceptions

Not every word ending with "ly" is an adverb.

Examples:

* Friendly (Adjective)
* Lovely (Adjective)
* Lonely (Adjective)

---

# 2️⃣ Adverb of Place

## 📖 Definition

Adverbs of place tell us **where an action happens**.

---

# Common Words

* Here
* There
* Everywhere
* Somewhere
* Inside
* Outside
* Nearby

---

# Examples

✅ Come here.

✅ He lives nearby.

✅ Look everywhere.

---

# 3️⃣ Adverb of Time

## 📖 Definition

Adverbs of time tell us **when an action happens**.

---

# Common Words

* Today
* Tomorrow
* Yesterday
* Now
* Soon
* Later
* Recently

---

# Examples

✅ I will meet you tomorrow.

✅ She arrived yesterday.

✅ He is working now.

---

# 4️⃣ Adverb of Frequency

## 📖 Definition

Adverbs of frequency show how often an action happens.

---

# Common Words

| Adverb    | Meaning      |
| --------- | ------------ |
| Always    | Every time   |
| Usually   | Most times   |
| Often     | Many times   |
| Sometimes | Occasionally |
| Rarely    | Not often    |
| Never     | No time      |

---

# Examples

✅ I always wake up early.

✅ She usually comes on time.

✅ They never lie.

---

# Position Rule

Frequency adverbs usually come:

Before main verb

Example:

✅ He always works hard.

---

After helping verb

Example:

✅ She is always happy.

---

# 5️⃣ Adverb of Degree

## 📖 Definition

Adverbs of degree show the intensity or level of an action or quality.

---

# Common Words

* Very
* Too
* Quite
* Almost
* Extremely
* Enough

---

# Examples

✅ She is very intelligent.

✅ The water is too hot.

✅ He is extremely talented.

---
