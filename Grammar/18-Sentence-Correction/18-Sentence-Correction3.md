# 💼 Sentence Correction in Professional Communication

Correct sentence structure is essential for resumes, emails, interviews, reports, and LinkedIn posts.

---

## Resume Example

❌ I am having knowledge of Java and Python.

✅ I have knowledge of Java and Python.

---

## Email Example

❌ Kindly revert back soon.

✅ Kindly reply at your earliest convenience.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Myself Gunjan Kumar Sah.

✅ My name is Gunjan Kumar Sah.

OR

✅ I am Gunjan Kumar Sah.

---

## LinkedIn Example

❌ Happy to announce I completed successfully internship.

✅ Happy to share that I successfully completed my internship.

# ⚠️ Common Sentence Correction Errors

### Error 1

❌ More better

✅ Better

---

### Error 2

❌ Return back

✅ Return

---

### Error 3

❌ Repeat again

✅ Repeat

---

### Error 4

❌ Discuss about

✅ Discuss

---

### Error 5

❌ Enter into the room

✅ Enter the room

# 📄 Sentence Correction Cheat Sheet

```text
SUBJECT–VERB AGREEMENT

He goes

They play

One of + Plural Noun + Singular Verb

---------------------------------

TENSE

Yesterday → Past

Today → Present

Tomorrow → Future

---------------------------------

ARTICLES

A → Consonant sound

An → Vowel sound

The → Specific noun

---------------------------------

PREPOSITIONS

Good at

Interested in

Married to

Discuss (No about)

Enter (No into)

---------------------------------

WORD ORDER

Subject + Verb + Object

---------------------------------

REDUNDANCY

Return back → Return

Repeat again → Repeat

More better → Better

Past history → History

---------------------------------

COMMON INTRODUCTION

❌ Myself Rahul.

✅ My name is Rahul.

✅ I am Rahul.
```
