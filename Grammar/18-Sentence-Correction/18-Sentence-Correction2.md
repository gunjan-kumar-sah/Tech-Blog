# 🔵 Modifier Errors

## 📖 What is a Modifier?

A **modifier** is a word or phrase that describes another word in a sentence.

A modifier should always be placed **close to the word it modifies**.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Walking down the street, the flowers looked beautiful.

### Why is it incorrect?

It incorrectly suggests that **the flowers were walking**.

---

## Correct

✅ Walking down the street, I noticed the beautiful flowers.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Covered in chocolate, Rahul ate the cake.

---

## Correct

✅ Rahul ate the cake that was covered in chocolate.

---

# 🟢 Parallelism

## 📖 Definition

Items in a list or comparison should follow the **same grammatical structure**.

---

## Incorrect

❌ She likes reading, to swim, and dancing.

---

## Correct

✅ She likes reading, swimming, and dancing.

---

## Incorrect

❌ The job requires communication skills, leadership, and to solve problems.

---

## Correct

✅ The job requires communication skills, leadership, and problem-solving skills.

---

# 🟣 Redundancy (Wordiness)

Avoid unnecessary words that repeat the same meaning.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Return back.

✅ Return.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Repeat again.

✅ Repeat.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Advance planning.

✅ Planning.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Past history.

✅ History.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Free gift.

✅ Gift.

---

# 🟡 Frequently Confused Words

| Incorrect Usage | Correct Usage |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Accept / Except | Accept = receive, Except = excluding |
| Affect / Effect | Affect = verb, Effect = noun |
| Advice / Advise | Advice = noun, Advise = verb |
| Lose / Loose | Lose = not win, Loose = not tight |
| Then / Than | Then = time, Than = comparison |
| Your / You're | Your = possession, You're = You are |
| Its / It's | Its = possession, It's = It is |

# 🔴 Comparison Errors

Use the correct comparative forms.

---

## Incorrect

❌ More better

---

## Correct

✅ Better

---

## Incorrect

❌ Most tallest

---

## Correct

✅ Tallest

---

## Incorrect

❌ More easier

---

## Correct

✅ Easier

---

# ⚪ Logical Consistency

Ensure that the sentence makes logical sense.

---

## Incorrect

❌ The book wrote the author.

---

## Correct

✅ The author wrote the book.

---

## Incorrect

❌ The car drove Rahul.

---

## Correct

✅ Rahul drove the car.

# 💼 Professional Writing Examples

## Resume

❌ I am having excellent communication skills.

✅ I have excellent communication skills.

---

## Email

❌ Kindly revert back soon.

✅ Kindly reply soon.

---

## Interview

❌ Myself Gunjan Kumar Sah.

✅ My name is Gunjan Kumar Sah.

OR

✅ I am Gunjan Kumar Sah.

---

## LinkedIn

❌ Happy to announce I successfully completed my internship with successfully.

# 📋 Sentence Correction Checklist

Before finalizing any sentence, ask yourself:

- ✔ Is the subject correct?
- ✔ Does the verb agree with the subject?
- ✔ Is the tense correct?
- ✔ Are articles used properly?
- ✔ Are prepositions correct?
- ✔ Is the punctuation correct?
- ✔ Is there any redundancy?
- ✔ Does the sentence sound natural?

# ⚠️ Common Errors

### Error 1

❌ Each of the boys are present.

✅ Each of the boys is present.

---

### Error 2

❌ Neither of them are ready.

✅ Neither of them is ready.

---

### Error 3

❌ I prefer tea than coffee.

✅ I prefer tea to coffee.

---

### Error 4

❌ He is good in Mathematics.

✅ He is good at Mathematics.

---

### Error 5

❌ She is married with him.

✅ She is married to him.
