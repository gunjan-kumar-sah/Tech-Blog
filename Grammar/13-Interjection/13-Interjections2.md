# 🎭 Formal vs Informal Interjections

Interjections are used differently depending on the situation.

| Formal English   | Informal English |
| ---------------- | ---------------- |
| Indeed!          | Wow!             |
| Certainly!       | Yeah!            |
| Congratulations! | Awesome!         |
| Excellent!       | Cool!            |
| Thank you!       | Thanks!          |
| I understand.    | Oh! Got it!      |

> **Tip:** Use **formal interjections** in exams, interviews, presentations, and professional emails. Use **informal interjections** in casual conversations with friends and family.

---

# 💬 Interjections in Everyday Conversations

### Example 1

👩 A: I got selected for the internship!

👨 B: **Wow!** Congratulations!

---

### Example 2

👩 A: I forgot my assignment at home.

👨 B: **Oops!** That happens sometimes.

---

### Example 3

👩 A: We won the coding competition.

👨 B: **Hurray!** That's fantastic!

---

### Example 4

👩 A: I accidentally deleted the file.

👨 B: **Oh no!** Do you have a backup?

---

### Example 5

👩 A: I scored 98% in the exam.

👨 B: **Excellent!** Keep it up.

# ✍️ Interjections in Writing

Interjections are commonly used in:

* Stories
* Dialogues
* Comics
* Social media posts
* Speeches
* Motivational content

### Example

> **Wow!** Your portfolio looks impressive.

> **Bravo!** You completed all the projects.

> **Oops!** I clicked the wrong button.

---

# 💼 Interjections in Professional Communication

Avoid excessive interjections in formal documents.

### ❌ Too Informal

> Wow! Our company achieved record profits!

### ✅ Professional

> We are delighted to announce that our company achieved record profits.

---

### ❌ Email Example

> Hey! Send me the report.

### ✅ Better

> Hello, could you please send me the report?

---

### ❌ Interview Example

> Wow! I know Python.

### ✅ Better

> I have strong knowledge of Python and enjoy building real-world projects.

---

# 📚 Interjections in Spoken English

Using interjections naturally makes conversations sound fluent.

### Surprise

**Wow!** That's amazing.

---

### Happiness

**Hurray!** We did it.

---

### Pain

**Ouch!** My hand hurts.

---

### Sympathy

**Oh no!** I'm sorry to hear that.

---

### Greeting

**Hello!** Nice to meet you.

---

# 📌 Punctuation Rules

## Rule 1: Strong Emotion

Use an **exclamation mark (!)**

✅ Wow! This is beautiful.

---

## Rule 2: Mild Emotion

Use a **comma (,)**

✅ Well, let's begin.

---

## Rule 3: Formal Writing

Avoid unnecessary interjections.

❌ Wow! The report was submitted.

✅ The report was submitted successfully.

---

# ⚠️ Common Errors

## Error 1

❌ Wow, Amazing!

✅ Wow! Amazing!

---

## Error 2

❌ Oops I forgot.

✅ Oops! I forgot.

---

## Error 3

❌ Hurray We won!

✅ Hurray! We won!

---

## Error 4

❌ Hello! Dear Sir!

✅ Hello, Sir.

---

## Error 5

❌ Bravo You did well.

✅ Bravo! You did well.

---

# 🎯 Placement Tips

* Learn the meaning of common interjections.
* Know when to use **!** and **,**.
* Avoid overusing interjections in formal English.
* Read dialogues to understand natural usage.
