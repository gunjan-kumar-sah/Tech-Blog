# 📖 What is an Interjection?

An **Interjection** is a word or short phrase that expresses a sudden feeling or emotion.

It is **not grammatically connected** to the rest of the sentence.

Interjections express emotions like:

* 😀 Joy
* 😲 Surprise
* 😢 Sadness
* 😨 Fear
* 😠 Anger
* 👏 Appreciation
* 🤔 Doubt
* 😌 Relief

---

# 🌍 Examples

### Example 1

> **Wow!** That's an amazing idea.

---

### Example 2

> **Oops!** I dropped my phone.

---

### Example 3

> **Hurray!** We won the match.

---

### Example 4

> **Alas!** The old tree has fallen.

# 🎯 Why Are Interjections Important?

Interjections help you:

* Express emotions naturally
* Improve spoken English
* Make conversations more engaging
* Write expressive dialogues and stories
* Sound more natural in everyday communication

# 😀 1. Interjections of Joy

Used to express happiness or excitement.

### Common Words

* Hurray!
* Yay!
* Bravo!
* Wonderful!
* Excellent!

---

### Examples

✅ Hurray! We won the championship.

✅ Bravo! You performed brilliantly.

---

# 😲 2. Interjections of Surprise

Used when something unexpected happens.

### Common Words

* Wow!
* Oh!
* What!
* Really!

---

### Examples

✅ Wow! Your project looks amazing.

✅ Oh! I didn't know that.

---

# 😢 3. Interjections of Sorrow

Express sadness or grief.

### Common Words

* Alas!
* Oh no!
* Sadly!

---

### Examples

✅ Alas! The king is dead.

✅ Oh no! I forgot my wallet.

---

# 👏 4. Interjections of Approval

Express appreciation or admiration.

### Common Words

* Well done!
* Excellent!
* Superb!
* Fantastic!

---

### Examples

✅ Excellent! Your presentation was outstanding.

✅ Well done! Keep it up.

---

# 👋 5. Interjections of Greeting

Used while greeting people.

### Common Words

* Hello!
* Hi!
* Welcome!
* Good morning!

---

### Examples

✅ Hello! How are you?

✅ Welcome! Please come in.

---

# 📢 6. Interjections for Attention

Used to call someone's attention.

### Common Words

* Hey!
* Listen!
* Look!
* Excuse me!

---

### Examples

✅ Hey! Wait for me.

✅ Excuse me! Can you help me?

---

# 🤕 7. Interjections of Pain

Express physical or emotional pain.

### Common Words

* Ouch!
* Ow!
* Ah!

---

### Examples

✅ Ouch! That really hurt.

✅ Ah! My head is aching.

---

# 📝 Rules for Using Interjections

### Rule 1

An interjection is usually placed at the **beginning** of a sentence.

✅ Wow! That is beautiful.

---

### Rule 2

Use an **exclamation mark (!)**
when the emotion is strong.

✅ Hurray! We won.

---

### Rule 3

Use a **comma (,)**
when the emotion is mild.

✅ Well, I think you're right.

---

### Rule 4

Do not overuse interjections in formal writing.

❌ Wow! Our company earned a profit!

✅ Our company achieved excellent financial results.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1

❌ Wow this is amazing

✅ Wow! This is amazing.

---

### Mistake 2

❌ Hurray, we won!

✅ Hurray! We won!

---

### Mistake 3

❌ Alas, he died.

✅ Alas! He died.
