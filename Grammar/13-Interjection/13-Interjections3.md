---

## Question 1

_____! We won the football match.

A. Alas

B. Hurray

C. Ouch

D. Oops

✅ **Answer:** **B. Hurray**

---

## Question 2

_____! I forgot my wallet.

A. Bravo

B. Oops

C. Hello

D. Well

✅ **Answer:** **B. Oops**

---

## Question 3

_____! That painting is beautiful.

A. Wow

B. Alas

C. Ouch

D. Ah

✅ **Answer:** **A. Wow**

---

## Question 4

_____! My hand is hurting.

A. Hurray

B. Ouch

C. Bravo

D. Welcome

✅ **Answer:** **B. Ouch**

---

## Question 5

_____! The old fort has collapsed.

A. Alas

Which interjection expresses appreciation?

A. Bravo!

B. Ouch!

✅ **Answer:** **A. Bravo!**

## Question 7

Which punctuation mark is generally used after a strong interjection?

# 🎯 Placement Level MCQs

### Q1. Which sentence is grammatically correct?

A. Oops I missed the train.

B. Oops! I missed the train.

C. Oops? I missed the train.

D. Oops. I missed train.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

### Q2. Choose the best interjection.

_____! You solved the coding challenge.

A. Bravo

B. Alas

C. Oops

D. Ouch

✅ **Answer:** **A**

---

### Q3. Which interjection expresses surprise?

A. Wow!

B. Alas!

C. Ouch!

D. Hurray!

✅ **Answer:** **A**

A. Wow! Your report is awesome!

B. Excellent work on the report. Thank you for your effort.

C. Cool! Nice report.

D. Yay! We completed it.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

✅ **Answer:** **B**

# 💼 Interjections in Professional Communication

Interjections should be used **carefully** in formal communication.

---

## Resume Example

❌ Wow! I have completed 20 projects.

✅ Successfully completed **20+ academic and personal projects**.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Wow! I love coding.

✅ I am passionate about software development and enjoy solving real-world problems.

---

## Workplace Example

❌ Hey! Finish this task now.

✅ Could you please complete this task by the end of the day?

---

## Email Example

❌ Oops! I forgot the attachment.

✅ I apologize for missing the attachment. Please find it attached now.

# ⚠️ Common Errors

### Error 1

❌ Wow this portfolio looks great.

✅ Wow! This portfolio looks great.

---

### Error 2

❌ Hurray, We won.

✅ Hurray! We won.

---

### Error 3

❌ Oops forgot my ID.

✅ Oops! I forgot my ID.

---

### Error 4

❌ Hello! Dear Sir,

✅ Dear Sir/Madam,

---

### Error 5

❌ Awesome! in a formal report.

✅ Use professional language instead of casual interjections.

```text id="interfinal"

INTERJECTIONS

Definition:
Express sudden feelings or emotions.


Common Interjections

😀 Joy
→ Hurray!

😲 Surprise
→ Wow!

😢 Sorrow
→ Alas!

👏 Appreciation
→ Bravo!

👋 Greeting
→ Hello!

🤕 Pain
→ Ouch!

😅 Mistake
→ Oops!


Punctuation

Strong Emotion
→ !

Mild Emotion
→ ,

Formal Writing
→ Use Sparingly

```
