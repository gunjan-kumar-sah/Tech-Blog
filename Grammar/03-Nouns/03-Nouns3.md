# 📝 Noun Practice Questions

Identify the type of noun in each sentence.

---

## Question 1

**India** is a beautiful country.

A. Common Noun
B. Proper Noun
C. Abstract Noun
D. Collective Noun

✅ **Answer:** B. Proper Noun

---

## Question 2

The **team** played very well.

A. Proper Noun
B. Material Noun
C. Collective Noun
D. Abstract Noun

✅ **Answer:** C. Collective Noun

---

## Question 3

**Honesty** is the best policy.

A. Abstract Noun
B. Common Noun
C. Proper Noun
D. Material Noun

✅ **Answer:** A. Abstract Noun

---

## Question 4

The ring is made of **gold**.

A. Proper Noun
B. Material Noun
C. Collective Noun
D. Countable Noun

✅ **Answer:** B. Material Noun

---

## Question 5

I bought three **books**.

A. Uncountable Noun
B. Abstract Noun
C. Countable Noun
D. Proper Noun

✅ **Answer:** C. Countable Noun

# ⚠️ Most Common Noun Mistakes

## 1. Incorrect Plural Forms

❌ Childs

✅ Children

---

## 2. Wrong Uncountable Usage

❌ Informations

✅ Information

---

## 3. Incorrect Advice Usage

❌ He gave me an advice.

✅ He gave me some advice.

---

## 4. Capitalization Mistake

❌ india is my country.

✅ India is my country.

# 📄 Noun Cheat Sheet

```text id="noun-cheat"
NOUN

Definition:
Name of person, place, thing, animal or idea.

Types:

1. Proper Noun
Specific name
Example: India, Rahul

2. Common Noun
General name
Example: Boy, City

3. Collective Noun
Group name
Example: Team, Army

4. Abstract Noun
Feeling/Quality
Example: Love, Honesty

5. Material Noun
Substance
Example: Gold, Water

6. Countable Noun
Can be counted
Example: Books

7. Uncountable Noun
Cannot be counted
Example: Information
```
