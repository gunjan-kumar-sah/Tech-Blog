# 📖 What is Subject-Verb Agreement?

**Subject-Verb Agreement** means that the subject and verb in a sentence must agree with each other in:

* Number (Singular/Plural)
* Person (First/Second/Third)

In simple words:

> **A singular subject takes a singular verb, and a plural subject takes a plural verb.**

---

# 🌍 Basic Examples

### Example 1

✅ He **plays** cricket.

❌ He **play** cricket.

Reason:

"He" is singular, so verb takes **s/es**.

---

### Example 2

✅ They **play** cricket.

❌ They **plays** cricket.

Reason:

"They" is plural, so use base verb.

---

# 🎯 Why is Subject-Verb Agreement Important?

Correct agreement helps in:

* Professional communication
* Grammar exams
* Interviews
* Writing emails
* Speaking confidently

# 🧩 Basic Rule

## Rule 1:

### Singular Subject + Singular Verb

Formula:

```text id="sva01"
Singular Subject + V1(s/es)
```

---

# Examples

| Subject | Verb   | Sentence            |
| ------- | ------ | ------------------- |
| He      | works  | He works hard       |
| She     | writes | She writes blogs    |
| Rahul   | plays  | Rahul plays cricket |

---

# Rule 2:

### Plural Subject + Plural Verb

Formula:

```text id="sva02"
Plural Subject + V1
```

---

# Examples

| Subject  | Verb  | Sentence               |
| -------- | ----- | ---------------------- |
| They     | work  | They work hard         |
| Students | study | Students study English |
| Players  | play  | Players play football  |

---

# 🧠 Memory Trick

Remember:

```text id="svatrick01"
He / She / It
       ↓
Verb + s/es


I / We / You / They
       ↓
Base Verb
```

# 1️⃣ Singular Subject Rules

## Rule 1:

Words like:

* He
* She
* It
* Rahul
* Student
* Teacher

take singular verbs.

---

Examples:

✅ Rahul **is** a student.

✅ She **likes** music.

✅ The dog **barks** loudly.

---

# Rule 2:

Singular indefinite pronouns take singular verbs.

Examples:

* Everyone
* Someone
* Nobody
* Anybody
* Somebody

---

Examples:

✅ Everyone **has** a dream.

❌ Everyone **have** a dream.

---

✅ Someone **is** calling you.

❌ Someone **are** calling you.

---

# Rule 3:

Words ending with "one", "body", "thing" are usually singular.

Examples:

| Word       | Verb   |
| ---------- | ------ |
| Everyone   | is/has |
| Someone    | is/has |
| Everything | is/has |
| Nothing    | is/has |

---

# 2️⃣ Plural Subject Rules

## Rule 4:

Plural subjects take plural verbs.

Examples:

✅ The boys **are** playing.

✅ The students **have** books.

---

Plural Subjects:

* Boys
* Girls
* Students
* Teachers
* Players
* People

---

# Rule 5:

Subjects joined by "and" usually take plural verbs.

Formula:

```text id="andrule01"
A + and + B = Plural
```

---

Examples:

✅ Rahul and Aman **are** friends.

✅ Tea and coffee **are** available.

---

# ⚠️ Exception

If two words represent one idea, use singular verb.

Examples:

✅ Bread and butter **is** my breakfast.

Reason:

Together they represent one item/idea.

---

# Rule 6:

Use "is" with singular and "are" with plural.

| Singular | Plural    |
| -------- | --------- |
| He is    | They are  |
| She is   | They are  |
| It is    | These are |
