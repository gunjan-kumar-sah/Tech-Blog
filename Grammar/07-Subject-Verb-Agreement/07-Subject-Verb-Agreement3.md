# 📝 Subject-Verb Agreement Practice Questions

Test your understanding with these practice questions.

---

## Question 1

She ___ to school every day.

A. go
B. goes
C. going
D. gone

✅ **Answer:** B. goes

**Explanation:**

"She" is a singular subject, so we use **goes**.

---

## Question 2

They ___ football every evening.

A. plays
B. play
C. playing
D. played

✅ **Answer:** B. play

**Explanation:**

"They" is plural, so we use the base verb.

---

## Question 3

# 💼 Subject-Verb Agreement in Interviews

Correct grammar creates a professional impression.

---

## ❌ Incorrect:

> My skills includes Python and Java.

## ✅ Correct:

> My skills include Python and Java.

---

## ❌ Incorrect:

> My project are completed.

## ✅ Correct:

> My project is completed.

---

## ❌ Incorrect:

> The experience I have are valuable.

## ✅ Correct:

> The experience I have is valuable.

---

# 🚀 Professional Communication Examples

## Resume Introduction

❌ Incorrect:

> I am a student who have knowledge of programming.

✅ Correct:

> I am a student who has knowledge of programming.

---

## Project Explanation

❌ Incorrect:

> My projects includes AI and Web Development.

✅ Correct:

> My projects include AI and Web Development.

# 📄 Subject-Verb Agreement Cheat Sheet

```text id="svafinal"

SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT

Basic Rule:

Singular Subject
        ↓
Singular Verb

Example:
He plays.


Plural Subject
        ↓
Plural Verb

Example:
They play.


Important Rules:

Each / Every
        ↓
Singular Verb


Everyone / Someone
        ↓
Singular Verb


A number of
        ↓
Plural Verb


The number of
        ↓
Singular Verb


Either / Neither
        ↓
Nearest Subject


Uncountable Nouns
        ↓
Singular Verb

```
