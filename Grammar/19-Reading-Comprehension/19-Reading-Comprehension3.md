# 📝 Reading Comprehension Practice Passage 1

## 📖 Passage

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries around the world. It helps automate repetitive tasks, improves decision-making through data analysis, and enhances customer experiences with chatbots and personalized recommendations. However, AI also raises concerns about data privacy, job displacement, and ethical decision-making. To maximize its benefits, organizations should use AI responsibly and invest in employee training.

---

### Question 1

What is the main idea of the passage?

A. AI only creates job losses.

B. AI has advantages as well as challenges.

C. AI is useful only in customer service.

D. AI is replacing all human workers.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Question 2

Which concern is mentioned in the passage?

A. High electricity consumption

B. Data privacy

C. Internet speed

D. Software licensing

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Question 3

What should organizations do?

A. Stop using AI

B. Ignore ethical issues

C. Use AI responsibly and train employees

D. Replace all workers

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

# 📝 Reading Comprehension Practice Passage 2

## 📖 Passage

Regular exercise improves both physical and mental health. It strengthens muscles, improves heart health, reduces stress, and increases energy levels. Experts recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity on most days of the week to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

---

### Question 1

What is the passage mainly about?

A. Sports competitions

B. Benefits of regular exercise

C. Heart diseases

D. Nutrition

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Question 2

How much exercise is recommended?

A. 10 minutes

B. 20 minutes

C. 30 minutes

D. 60 minutes

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Question 3

According to the passage, exercise helps:

A. Increase stress

B. Improve health

C. Reduce sleep

D. Cause illness

✅ **Answer:** **B**

A. Scanning

A. Skimming

B. Scanning

A. Opinion

B. Assumption

# 💼 Reading Comprehension in Interviews

Interviewers may provide short passages to evaluate:

- Reading speed
- Understanding
- Analytical thinking
- Communication skills
- Logical reasoning

### Tips

- Read carefully before answering.
- Support answers with evidence from the passage.
- Avoid assumptions.
- Keep answers clear and concise.

```text
READING COMPREHENSION

Read the Title

Skim for Main Idea

Understand Vocabulary

↓

↓

Identify Purpose
```
