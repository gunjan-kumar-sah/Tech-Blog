# ❓ Placement MCQs

## Question 1

Which sentence is grammatically correct?

A. He go to school.

B. He goes to school.

C. He going to school.

D. He gone to school.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

## Question 2

Which sentence is correct?

A. They is playing.

B. They are playing.

C. They am playing.

D. They was playing.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

## Question 3

Choose the correct punctuation.

A. What is your name

B. What is your name.

C. What is your name?

D. What is your name!

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

## Question 4

Which sentence starts correctly?

A. my name is Rahul.

B. My Name is Rahul.

C. My name is Rahul.

D. my Name is Rahul.

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

## Question 5

Every sentence must contain:

A. Subject only

B. Verb only

C. Subject and Verb

D. Object only

✅ **Answer:** **C**

# ⚠️ Common Grammar Mistakes

Avoid these mistakes:

* ❌ Missing articles (**a, an, the**)
* ❌ Wrong verb forms
* ❌ Incorrect subject–verb agreement
* ❌ Missing punctuation
* ❌ Capitalization errors
* ❌ Incomplete sentences
* ❌ Mixing tenses
