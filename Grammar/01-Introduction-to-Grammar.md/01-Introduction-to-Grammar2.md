# 🏗️ Structure of a Sentence

Every correct English sentence is built using a proper structure.

### Basic Formula

```text
Subject + Verb + Object
```

### Example

| Subject | Verb  | Object  |
| ------- | ----- | ------- |
| Rahul   | plays | Cricket |
| She     | reads | Books   |
| They    | watch | Movies  |
| We      | learn | English |
| I       | write | Notes   |

---

# 📖 What is a Sentence?

A **sentence** is a group of words that expresses a complete thought.

A sentence must have:

* ✅ Subject
* ✅ Verb
* ✅ Complete Meaning

### Correct Examples

* She is a doctor.
* We are learning grammar.
* They play football.

### Incorrect Examples

* Playing football.
* My friend.
* Reading a book.

# 🧠 Grammar vs Vocabulary

Many beginners confuse Grammar and Vocabulary.

| Grammar                       | Vocabulary                  |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Rules of a language           | Words of a language         |
| Helps form correct sentences  | Helps improve communication |
| Focuses on sentence structure | Focuses on word knowledge   |

### Example

Vocabulary:

> Beautiful

Grammar:

> She is a beautiful girl.

# 📚 Types of Sentences

There are four main types of sentences.

---

## 1️⃣ Declarative Sentence

Used to give information.

Example:

* I live in India.
* She is a teacher.

---

## 2️⃣ Interrogative Sentence

Used to ask questions.

Example:

* Where do you live?
* What is your name?

---

## 3️⃣ Imperative Sentence

Used to give commands, requests, or advice.

Example:

* Please open the door.
* Study regularly.
* Be honest.

---

## 4️⃣ Exclamatory Sentence

Shows strong feelings or emotions.

Example:

* What a beautiful day!
* Wow!
* Congratulations!

---

# 📝 Sentence Formation Tips

✔ Begin with a capital letter.

✔ Use the correct verb.

✔ Follow Subject–Verb Agreement.

✔ End with proper punctuation.

✔ Make sure the sentence expresses a complete idea.

# 💡 Golden Grammar Rules

## Rule 1

Every sentence starts with a capital letter.

---

## Rule 2

Every sentence ends with punctuation.

---

## Rule 3

A verb is compulsory.

---

## Rule 4

The subject and verb must agree.

---

## Rule 5

Keep the tense consistent throughout the sentence.

---

# ⚡ Quick Grammar Tricks

### Trick 1

If the subject is singular,

➡️ use a singular verb.

Example:

He **plays** football.

---

### Trick 2

If the subject is plural,

➡️ use a plural verb.

Example:

They **play** football.

---

### Trick 3

Always read the sentence aloud.

If it sounds awkward, check:

* Subject
* Verb
* Tense
* Punctuation

---

# ❌ Common Beginner Errors

* Writing without punctuation.
* Forgetting helping verbs.
* Mixing singular and plural forms.
* Incorrect capitalization.
* Using incomplete sentences.
