# 🔥 Adjective Order Rules

## 📖 Definition

When we use multiple adjectives before a noun, they usually follow a specific order.

The correct order is:

```text id="adjorder01"

Opinion
↓
Size
↓
Age
↓
Shape
↓
Color
↓
Origin
↓
Material
↓
Noun

```

---

# Examples

❌ Incorrect:

> A red beautiful car

✅ Correct:

> A beautiful red car

---

# Example 2

❌ Incorrect:

> A wooden old table

✅ Correct:

> An old wooden table

---

# Complete Adjective Order

| Order | Type     | Example   |
| ----- | -------- | --------- |
| 1     | Opinion  | Beautiful |
| 2     | Size     | Big       |
| 3     | Age      | Old       |
| 4     | Shape    | Round     |
| 5     | Color    | Red       |
| 6     | Origin   | Indian    |
| 7     | Material | Wooden    |
| 8     | Noun     | Table     |

---

# Example Sentence

> She bought a beautiful small old round red Indian wooden box.

Adjective order:

Beautiful → Opinion

Small → Size

Old → Age

Round → Shape

Red → Color

Indian → Origin

Wooden → Material

Box → Noun

# ⚠️ Common Adjective Mistakes

---

## Mistake 1

❌ He is enough smart.

✅ He is smart enough.

---

## Mistake 2

❌ She is a girl beautiful.

✅ She is a beautiful girl.

---

## Mistake 3

❌ This is the most unique idea.

✅ This is a unique idea.

---

## Mistake 4

❌ He is more better than me.

✅ He is better than me.

---

## Mistake 5

❌ She is elder than five years.

✅ She is five years older.

# 📝 Adjective Practice Questions

Test your understanding.

---

## Question 1

He is a ___ boy.

A. intelligence

B. intelligent

C. intelligently

D. intelligencey

✅ Answer: B. intelligent

---

## Question 2

This is the ___ building in the city.

A. tall

B. taller

C. tallest

D. more tall

✅ Answer: C. tallest

---

## Question 3

She is ___ than her sister.

A. beautiful

B. more beautiful

C. most beautiful

D. beautifuller

✅ Answer: B. more beautiful

---

## Question 4

Rahul is the ___ student in the class.

A. good

B. better

C. best

D. goodest

✅ Answer: C. best

---

## Question 5

I have ___ money.

A. many

B. much

C. few

D. several

✅ Answer: B. much

Types:

1. Quality
Beautiful, Smart


2. Quantity
Some, Little


3. Number
Two, Many


4. Demonstrative
This, That


5. Possessive
My, Your


6. Interrogative
Which, What

Positive:
Tall

Taller


Superlative:
Tallest


Irregular:

Good

Better
↓
Best
