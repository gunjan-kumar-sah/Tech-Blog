# 📖 What is an Adjective?

An **Adjective** is a word that describes or modifies a:

* Noun
* Pronoun

In simple words:

> **An adjective tells us more information about a person, place, animal, or thing.**

---

# 🌍 Examples

### Example 1

> She is a **beautiful** girl.

Beautiful describes the girl.

---

### Example 2

> He has a **new** laptop.

New describes the laptop.

---

### Example 3

> I live in a **large** house.

Large describes the house.

---

# 🎯 Why Are Adjectives Important?

Adjectives help us:

* Describe things clearly
* Improve communication
* Make sentences attractive
* Write professional content
* Express ideas better

---

# 🔍 How to Identify an Adjective?

Ask these questions:

### Question 1:

**What kind?**

Example:

> He is an intelligent student.

What kind of student?

Answer:

Intelligent → Adjective

---

### Question 2:

**How many?**

Example:

> I have three books.

How many books?

Answer:

Three → Adjective

---

### Question 3:

**How much?**

Example:

> There is little water.

How much water?

Answer:

Little → Adjective

# 1️⃣ Adjective of Quality

## 📖 Definition

Adjective of Quality describes the quality or nature of a noun.

It answers:

> What kind?

---

# Examples

| Sentence                  | Adjective   |
| ------------------------- | ----------- |
| She is a smart girl.      | Smart       |
| He is an honest person.   | Honest      |
| It is a beautiful flower. | Beautiful   |
| Rahul is hardworking.     | Hardworking |

---

# More Examples

✅ A brave soldier

✅ A tall building

✅ A fast car

✅ A difficult problem

---

# 2️⃣ Adjective of Quantity

## 📖 Definition

Adjective of Quantity tells us the amount of something.

It answers:

> How much?

---

# Examples

| Sentence                 | Adjective |
| ------------------------ | --------- |
| I have some money.       | Some      |
| There is little water.   | Little    |
| He has enough knowledge. | Enough    |

---

# Common Words

* Some
* Much
* Little
* Enough
* Sufficient
* Whole

---

# Examples

✅ There is enough food.

✅ I have little time.

✅ She has much patience.

---

# 3️⃣ Adjective of Number

## 📖 Definition

Adjective of Number tells the number or order of nouns.

It answers:

> How many?

---

# Types of Adjective of Number

## 1. Definite Number

Exact number.

Examples:

* One
* Two
* Three
* Ten

Sentence:

> I have two brothers.

---

## 2. Indefinite Number

Not exact.

Examples:

* Many
* Few
* Several
* Some

Sentence:

> Many students attended the class.

---

## 3. Distributive Number

Shows individual members.

Examples:

* Each
* Every
* Either
* Neither

---

Examples:

✅ Each student received a certificate.

✅ Every player worked hard.

---

# 4️⃣ Demonstrative Adjective

## 📖 Definition

Demonstrative adjectives point out specific nouns.

---

# Common Words

* This
* That
* These
* Those

---

# Examples

✅ This book is interesting.

✅ That car is expensive.

✅ These students are talented.

✅ Those buildings are old.

---

# 5️⃣ Possessive Adjective

## 📖 Definition

Possessive adjectives show ownership or belonging.

---

# Common Words

* My
* Our
* Your
* His
* Her
* Its
* Their

---

# Examples

✅ My laptop is new.

✅ Her dress is beautiful.

✅ Their project is completed.
