---

# 7. Past Perfect Tense

## 📖 Definition

Past Perfect Tense shows an action that was completed **before another action in the past**.

---

# Formula

```text id="pastperfect01"
Subject + had + V3 + Object
```

---

# Examples

✅ I had completed my homework before dinner.

✅ She had left before I arrived.

✅ They had finished the project.

---

# Negative Sentence

Formula:

```text id="pastperfect02"
Subject + had not + V3 + Object
```

Example:

> He had not completed the work.

---

# Question Sentence

Formula:

```text id="pastperfect03"
Had + Subject + V3 + Object?
```

Example:

> Had you finished your assignment?

---

# 8. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

## 📖 Definition

Past Perfect Continuous describes an action that continued for some time before another past action.

---

# Formula

```text id="pastperfect04"
Subject + had been + V1+ing + Object + since/for
```

---

# Examples

✅ I had been studying for three hours.

✅ She had been working since morning.

✅ They had been waiting for a long time.

---

# 🚀 Future Tense

## 📖 Definition

Future Tense describes an action that will happen later.

---

# Types of Future Tense

1. Simple Future Tense

2. Future Continuous Tense

3. Future Perfect Tense

4. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

---

# 9. Simple Future Tense

## 📖 Definition

Used for actions that will happen in the future.

---

# Formula

```text id="future01"
Subject + will + V1 + Object
```

---

# Examples

✅ I will learn Python.

✅ She will join the meeting.

✅ They will complete the project.

---

# Negative Sentence

Formula:

```text id="future02"
Subject + will not + V1 + Object
```

Example:

> I will not go there.

---

# Question Sentence

Formula:

```text id="future03"
Will + Subject + V1 + Object?
```

Example:

> Will you help me?

---

# 10. Future Continuous Tense

## 📖 Definition

Future Continuous shows an action that will be happening at a specific time in the future.

---

# Formula

```text id="future04"
Subject + will be + V1+ing + Object
```

---

# Examples

✅ I will be studying tomorrow evening.

✅ She will be working at 10 AM.

---

# 11. Future Perfect Tense

## 📖 Definition

Future Perfect shows an action that will be completed before a certain future time.

---

# Formula

```text id="future05"
Subject + will have + V3 + Object
```

---

# Examples

✅ I will have completed my project by Monday.

✅ She will have reached home.

---

# 12. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

## 📖 Definition

Future Perfect Continuous shows an action that will continue for a duration until a future point.

---

# Formula

```text id="future06"
Subject + will have been + V1+ing + since/for
```

---

# Examples

✅ I will have been working for five hours.

✅ They will have been studying since morning.

---
