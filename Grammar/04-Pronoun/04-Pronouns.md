# 📖 What is a Pronoun?

A **Pronoun** is a word that is used **instead of a noun** to avoid repetition.

In simple words:

> **A pronoun replaces a noun in a sentence.**

---

# 🌍 Example Without Pronoun

❌ Rahul is a student. Rahul studies computer science. Rahul likes programming.

The name "Rahul" is repeated many times.

---

# ✅ Example With Pronoun

✔ Rahul is a student. **He** studies computer science. **He** likes programming.

Here:

**He** replaces **Rahul**.

---

# 🎯 Why Do We Use Pronouns?

Pronouns make sentences:

* ✅ Shorter
* ✅ Clearer
* ✅ More natural
* ✅ Easier to understand

---

# 🧩 Importance of Pronouns

Pronouns help us:

* Avoid repetition.
* Improve sentence flow.
* Make communication professional.
* Write better paragraphs.

---

# 📚 Types of Pronouns

English Grammar has several types of pronouns:

| No. | Type                  | Function                              |
| :-: | --------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
|  1  | Personal Pronoun      | Refers to people/things               |
|  2  | Possessive Pronoun    | Shows ownership                       |
|  3  | Demonstrative Pronoun | Points something                      |
|  4  | Reflexive Pronoun     | Refers back to subject                |
|  5  | Relative Pronoun      | Connects clauses                      |
|  6  | Interrogative Pronoun | Used for questions                    |
|  7  | Indefinite Pronoun    | Refers to non-specific persons/things |
|  8  | Reciprocal Pronoun    | Shows mutual action                   |

# 1️⃣ Personal Pronouns

## 📖 Definition

Personal Pronouns refer to specific people, animals, or things.

They are divided into:

* First Person
* Second Person
* Third Person

---

# 👤 First Person Pronouns

Used when the speaker talks about himself/herself.

### Examples:

| Singular | Plural |
| -------- | ------ |
| I        | We     |
| Me       | Us     |
| My       | Our    |
| Mine     | Ours   |

---

### Sentences:

✅ I am learning English.

Here:

I → First Person Pronoun

---

✅ We are preparing for placements.

Here:

We → First Person Pronoun

---

# 👥 Second Person Pronouns

Used when speaking to another person.

Examples:

* You
* Your
* Yours

---

### Sentences:

✅ You are my friend.

Here:

You → Second Person Pronoun

---

✅ Your book is on the table.

Your → Possessive form

---

# 👨 Third Person Pronouns

Used for people or things being discussed.

Examples:

| Male | Female | Things |
| ---- | ------ | ------ |
| He   | She    | It     |
| Him  | Her    | Its    |
| His  | Hers   | —      |
| They | Them   | —      |

---

### Sentences:

✅ He is a developer.

He → Third Person Pronoun

---

✅ She is a teacher.

She → Third Person Pronoun

---

✅ It is a beautiful phone.

It → Third Person Pronoun

---

# 🧠 Memory Trick

Remember:

```text id="pronoun01"
1st Person → Speaker

2nd Person → Listener

3rd Person → Someone Else
```

Example:

```text
I → I speak

You → I talk to you

He/She → I talk about someone
```

# 2️⃣ Subject Pronouns

## 📖 Definition

Subject pronouns perform the action in a sentence.

### List:

| Subject Pronouns |
| ---------------- |
| I                |
| We               |
| You              |
| He               |
| She              |
| It               |
| They             |

---

# 📝 Examples

✅ I play cricket.

(I performs action)

---

✅ They study together.

(They performs action)

---

# 3️⃣ Object Pronouns

## 📖 Definition

Object pronouns receive the action of the verb.

### List:

| Subject | Object |
| ------- | ------ |
| I       | Me     |
| We      | Us     |
| You     | You    |
| He      | Him    |
| She     | Her    |
| It      | It     |
| They    | Them   |

---

# 📝 Examples

✅ She called me.

Me → Object Pronoun

---

✅ I helped him.

Him → Object Pronoun
