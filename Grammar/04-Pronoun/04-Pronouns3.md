# 📝 Pronoun Practice Questions

Identify the type of pronoun used in the sentence.

---

## Question 1

**He** is my best friend.

A. Possessive Pronoun
B. Personal Pronoun
C. Reflexive Pronoun
D. Relative Pronoun

✅ **Answer:** B. Personal Pronoun

---

## Question 2

This laptop is **mine**.

A. Demonstrative Pronoun
B. Interrogative Pronoun
C. Possessive Pronoun
D. Indefinite Pronoun

✅ **Answer:** C. Possessive Pronoun

---

## Question 3

**Who** is your teacher?

A. Relative Pronoun
B. Interrogative Pronoun
C. Personal Pronoun
D. Reflexive Pronoun

✅ **Answer:** B. Interrogative Pronoun

---

## Question 4

She completed the work **herself**.

A. Reflexive Pronoun
B. Possessive Pronoun
C. Demonstrative Pronoun
D. Reciprocal Pronoun

✅ **Answer:** A. Reflexive Pronoun

---

## Question 5

The boy **who** won the race is my brother.

A. Personal Pronoun
B. Relative Pronoun
C. Demonstrative Pronoun
D. Indefinite Pronoun

✅ **Answer:** B. Relative Pronoun

# 🎯 Placement MCQs

## Q1. A pronoun is used instead of:

A. Verb
B. Adjective
C. Noun
D. Adverb

✅ Answer: C

---

## Q2. Choose the correct pronoun:

> Rahul is a good student. ___ studies regularly.

A. He
B. Him
C. His
D. Himself

✅ Answer: A

---

## Q3. Which is a Possessive Pronoun?

A. They
B. Mine
C. This
D. Who

✅ Answer: B

---

## Q4. Which pronoun shows ownership?

A. Reflexive
B. Possessive
C. Relative
D. Interrogative

✅ Answer: B

---

## Q5. Identify the Demonstrative Pronoun:

A. She
B. Those
C. Myself
D. Who

✅ Answer: B

---

## Q6. Choose the correct sentence:

A. Myself will do it.

B. I will do it myself.

C. Myself did it.

D. Me will do it.

✅ Answer: B

## Q7. Which word is an Indefinite Pronoun?

A. Everyone
B. He
C. This
D. Which

✅ Answer: A

---

## Q8. "Each other" is a:

A. Personal Pronoun

B. Reciprocal Pronoun

C. Relative Pronoun

D. Possessive Pronoun

✅ Answer: B

## Q9. Find the pronoun:

# 💼 Placement & Interview Tips

Pronouns are commonly asked in:

* Error Detection
* Sentence Correction
* Fill in the Blanks
* Spoken English Tests
* Communication Rounds

### Interview Tip:

While speaking professionally:

✅ Avoid unnecessary repetition of nouns.

Example:

❌ Gunjan is a developer. Gunjan likes coding.

✅ Gunjan is a developer. He likes coding.

# ⚠️ Common Pronoun Mistakes

## 1. Wrong Subject Pronoun

❌ Me and Rahul completed the project.

✅ Rahul and I completed the project.

---

## 2. Wrong Possessive Form

❌ This book is her's.

✅ This book is hers.

---

## 3. Wrong Reflexive Usage

❌ Myself will attend the meeting.

✅ I will attend the meeting myself.

---

## 4. Incorrect Agreement

❌ Everyone have submitted their work.

✅ Everyone has submitted their work.

---

# 📄 Pronoun Cheat Sheet

```text id="pronoun-final"
PRONOUN

Definition:
A word used instead of a noun.

Types:

1. Personal Pronoun
→ I, You, He, She, They

2. Possessive Pronoun
→ Mine, Yours, Hers

3. Demonstrative Pronoun
→ This, That, These, Those

4. Reflexive Pronoun
→ Myself, Himself, Themselves

5. Relative Pronoun
→ Who, Which, Whose

6. Interrogative Pronoun
→ Who, What, Which

7. Indefinite Pronoun
→ Someone, Everyone

8. Reciprocal Pronoun
→ Each other, One another
```
