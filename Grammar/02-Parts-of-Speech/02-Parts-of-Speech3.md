# 🎯 Placement MCQs

### Q1.

Which Part of Speech replaces a noun?

A. Verb

B. Adverb

C. Pronoun

D. Adjective

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q2.

Which word joins two clauses?

A. Adverb

B. Conjunction

C. Pronoun

D. Verb

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q3.

Choose the adjective in the sentence:

> She is an intelligent student.

A. She

B. Intelligent

C. Student

D. Is

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q4.

Which word expresses emotion?

A. Verb

B. Interjection

C. Noun

D. Preposition

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q5.

Identify the verb:

> They are studying English.

A. They

B. English

C. Studying

D. Are

✅ **Answer:** **C** *(Main action verb; "are" is a helping verb.)*

---

### Q6.

Choose the correct sentence.

A. She go to school.

B. She goes to school.

C. She going school.

D. She gone school.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q7.

Which sentence contains a conjunction?

A. The dog is under the chair.

B. Rahul and Mohan are friends.

C. Wow! That's amazing.

D. She writes neatly.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q8.

Identify the preposition.

> We walked through the park.

A. Walked

B. Through

C. Park

D. We

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q9.

Choose the adverb.

> He completed the task quickly.

A. He

B. Task

C. Completed

D. Quickly

✅ **Answer:** **D**

---

### Q10.

How many Parts of Speech are there in traditional English grammar?

A. 5

# 💼 Placement & Interview Tips

Many placement exams test Parts of Speech through:

* Spotting Errors
* Fill in the Blanks
* Sentence Improvement
* Reading Comprehension
* Para Jumbles
* Grammar-based MCQs

### 💡 Pro Tip

Before answering any grammar question:

1. Find the **Verb**.
2. Identify the **Subject**.
3. Look for describing words (**Adjectives/Adverbs**).
4. Check connectors (**Conjunctions**) and relationship words (**Prepositions**).

This approach improves both speed and accuracy.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

Avoid these frequent errors:

* ❌ Confusing adjectives and adverbs.
* ❌ Using the wrong preposition.
* ❌ Missing helping verbs.
* ❌ Incorrect subject–verb agreement.
* ❌ Misusing pronouns.
* ❌ Joining sentences without proper conjunctions.

---

# 📄 Quick Revision Cheat Sheet

```text id="speech03"
8 Parts of Speech

1. Noun         → Name
2. Pronoun      → Replaces a noun
3. Verb         → Action / State
4. Adjective    → Describes a noun
5. Adverb       → Describes a verb, adjective, or adverb
6. Preposition  → Shows relationship
7. Conjunction  → Joins words or clauses
8. Interjection → Expresses emotion
```
