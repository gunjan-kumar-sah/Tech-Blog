# 📝 Articles Practice Questions

Test your knowledge of **A, An, The**.

---

## Question 1

He is ___ honest person.

A. a
B. an
C. the
D. no article

✅ **Answer:** B. an

**Explanation:**

"Honest" has a silent **h**, so it starts with a vowel sound.

---

## Question 2

I saw ___ elephant in the zoo.

A. a
B. an
C. the
D. no article

✅ **Answer:** B. an

---

## Question 3

___ sun rises in the east.

A. A
B. An
C. The
D. No article

✅ **Answer:** C. The

**Explanation:**

Sun is a unique object.

---

## Question 4

She is ___ university student.

A. an
B. a
C. the
D. no article

✅ **Answer:** B. a

**Explanation:**

University starts with "yu" sound (consonant sound).

---

## Question 5

I visited ___ Taj Mahal last year.

✅ **Answer:** C. The

# 🎯 Placement Level MCQs

## Q1. Choose the correct sentence:

A. He is an university student.

B. He is a university student.

C. He is the university student.

D. He university student.

✅ Answer: B

---

## Q2. Choose the correct sentence:

A. The India is beautiful.

B. India is beautiful.

C. An India is beautiful.

D. A India is beautiful.

✅ Answer: B

---

## Q3. Which article is used before vowel sound?

A. A

B. An

C. The

D. None

✅ Answer: B

# 💼 Articles in Professional Communication

Correct article usage improves your:

* Resume writing
* Emails
* Interviews
* Documentation
* Presentations

---

# Resume Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I am software developer.

✅ Correct:

> I am a software developer.

---

# Interview Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I completed the project in university.

✅ Correct:

> I completed the project in the university.

---

# Professional Introduction

Correct:

> I am a B.Tech Computer Science student with an interest in software development and artificial intelligence.

# ⚠️ Common Errors to Avoid

## Error 1

❌ She is a honest girl.

✅ She is an honest girl.

---

## Error 2

❌ He is the engineer.

(When talking generally)

✅ He is an engineer.

---

## Error 3

❌ I went to the school yesterday.

(General purpose)

✅ I went to school yesterday.

---

## Error 4

❌ The English is difficult.

✅ English is difficult.

# 📄 Articles Cheat Sheet

```text id="articlefinal"

ARTICLES

A

Used for:
- Singular countable nouns
- Consonant sound
- General meaning


Examples:

a book
a car
a university


--------------------


AN

Used for:
- Vowel sound
- Silent h words


Examples:

an apple
an hour
an honest man


--------------------


THE

Used for:

- Specific things
- Unique things
- Rivers
- Oceans
- Famous buildings
- Superlative degree


Examples:

the sun
the Ganga
the Taj Mahal


--------------------


NO ARTICLE

Used for:

- Names
- Languages
- Sports
- Meals


Examples:

India
English
Cricket
Breakfast

```
