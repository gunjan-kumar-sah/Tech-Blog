# 3️⃣ Rules of "The"

## 📖 Definition

"The" is a **definite article** used when we talk about a specific person, place, thing, or idea.

It tells that the listener already knows what we are talking about.

---

# Rule 1: Use "The" Before Unique Things

Some things are one of their kind.

Examples:

✅ The sun

✅ The moon

✅ The earth

✅ The sky

✅ The world

---

Sentences:

> The sun gives us light.

> The earth revolves around the sun.

---

# Rule 2: Use "The" Before Specific Things

When something is already mentioned or specific.

---

Example:

First time:

> I bought a laptop.

(Any laptop)

Second time:

> The laptop is very expensive.

(Specific laptop)

---

# Rule 3: Use "The" Before Superlative Degree

Superlative words show the highest degree.

Examples:

* Best
* Biggest
* Tallest
* Most beautiful
* Fastest

---

Examples:

✅ He is the best player.

✅ Mount Everest is the highest mountain.

✅ This is the most beautiful place.

---

# Rule 4: Use "The" Before Ordinal Numbers

Ordinal numbers:

* First
* Second
* Third
* Last
* Next

---

Examples:

✅ The first chapter is easy.

✅ The second question is difficult.

✅ The last page is important.

---

# Rule 5: Use "The" With Rivers

Names of rivers usually take "The".

---

Examples:

✅ The Ganga

✅ The Yamuna

✅ The Nile

✅ The Amazon

---

Sentences:

> The Ganga is a holy river.

---

# Rule 6: Use "The" With Seas and Oceans

Examples:

✅ The Indian Ocean

✅ The Pacific Ocean

✅ The Arabian Sea

---

# Rule 7: Use "The" With Mountain Ranges

Examples:

✅ The Himalayas

✅ The Alps

✅ The Andes

---

# Important Exception

Single mountains do not use "The".

Correct:

✅ Mount Everest

Incorrect:

❌ The Mount Everest

---

# Rule 8: Use "The" With Deserts

Examples:

✅ The Sahara Desert

✅ The Thar Desert

---

# Rule 9: Use "The" With Groups of Islands

Examples:

✅ The Maldives

✅ The Andaman and Nicobar Islands

---

# Rule 10: Use "The" With Newspapers

Examples:

✅ The Times of India

✅ The Hindu

✅ The New York Times

---

# Rule 11: Use "The" With Historical Buildings

Examples:

✅ The Taj Mahal

✅ The Red Fort

✅ The Qutub Minar

---

# Rule 12: Use "The" With Directions

Directions:

* East
* West
* North
* South

---

Examples:

✅ The East

✅ The West

✅ The North

---

Sentence:

> The sun rises in the east.

---

# 4️⃣ When NOT to Use Articles

Sometimes we do not use:

A / An / The

This is called **Zero Article**.

---

# Rule 13: Do Not Use Articles Before Names

Names of:

* People
* Cities
* Countries

usually don't take articles.

---

Examples:

✅ Rahul is my friend.

❌ The Rahul is my friend.

---

✅ India is a beautiful country.

❌ The India is a beautiful country.

---

# Exceptions:

Some countries use "The".

Examples:

✅ The United States

✅ The United Kingdom

✅ The Netherlands

---

# Rule 14: Do Not Use Articles Before Languages

Examples:

✅ I speak English.

❌ I speak the English.

---

Languages:

* English
* Hindi
* French
* Spanish

---

# Rule 15: Do Not Use Articles Before Subjects

Examples:

✅ Mathematics is interesting.

❌ The Mathematics is interesting.

---

Subjects:

* Physics
* Chemistry
* Biology
* Computer Science

---

# Rule 16: Do Not Use Articles Before Sports

Examples:

✅ I play cricket.

❌ I play the cricket.

---

Sports:

* Football
* Cricket
* Tennis
* Basketball

---

# Rule 17: Do Not Use Articles Before Meals

Examples:

✅ I had breakfast.

❌ I had the breakfast.

---

Meals:

* Breakfast
* Lunch
* Dinner

---

# ⚖️ A/An/The Comparison

| Sentence               | Meaning           |
| ---------------------- | ----------------- |
| I saw a dog            | Any dog           |
| I saw the dog          | Specific dog      |
| She is an engineer     | One engineer      |
| The engineer helped me | Specific engineer |

---

# ⚠️ Common Article Mistakes

## Mistake 1

❌ He is best student.

✅ He is the best student.

---

## Mistake 2

❌ Ganga is a holy river.

✅ The Ganga is a holy river.

---

## Mistake 3

❌ I live in the India.

✅ I live in India.

---

## Mistake 4

❌ He plays the cricket.

✅ He plays cricket.

---

# 🧠 Easy Memory Trick

```text id="article-rule"

A / An

→ One thing
→ General meaning


The

→ Specific thing
→ Unique thing
→ Famous places


No Article

→ Names
→ Languages
→ Sports
→ Meals

```
