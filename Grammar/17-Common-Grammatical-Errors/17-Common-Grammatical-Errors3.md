C. Myself is Gunjan.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

A. Return back soon.

C. Kindly revert back.

D. Please return soon.

A. More better

# 💼 Common Grammar Errors in Professional Communication

Correct grammar creates a positive impression in resumes, interviews, emails, and LinkedIn posts.

---

## Resume Example

❌ I am having knowledge of Python.

✅ I have knowledge of Python.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Myself Gunjan Kumar Sah.

✅ My name is Gunjan Kumar Sah.

OR

✅ I am Gunjan Kumar Sah.

---

## Email Example

❌ Kindly revert back soon.

✅ Kindly reply at your earliest convenience.

---

## LinkedIn Example

❌ I completed successfully my internship.

✅ I successfully completed my internship.

# 📄 Grammar Error Cheat Sheet

```text id="grammar17final"

SUBJECT–VERB AGREEMENT

He goes

They play

One of + Plural Noun + Singular Verb


TENSE

Yesterday → Past Tense

Now → Present Tense

Tomorrow → Future Tense


ARTICLES

a → consonant sound

an → vowel sound

the → specific noun


PREPOSITIONS

Good at

Interested in

Married to

Discuss (no about)

Enter (no into)


WORD ORDER

Subject + Verb + Object


DOUBLE NEGATIVE

Incorrect:
I don't know nothing.

Correct:
I don't know anything.


COMMON CORRECTIONS

Return back → Return

Repeat again → Repeat

More better → Better

Myself Rahul → My name is Rahul.

```
