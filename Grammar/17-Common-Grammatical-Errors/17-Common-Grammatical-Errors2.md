# 🟠 Article Errors

Articles (**a, an, the**) are often used incorrectly.

## Incorrect

❌ He is engineer.

## Correct

✅ He is **an** engineer.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Sun rises in east.

## Correct

✅ **The** Sun rises in **the** east.

---

## Incorrect

❌ I bought apple.

## Correct

✅ I bought **an** apple.

---

## Rules

* **A** → Before consonant sounds.
* **An** → Before vowel sounds.
* **The** → For specific persons, places, or things.

---

# 🔵 Preposition Errors

A preposition shows the relationship between words.

## Incorrect

❌ Discuss about the problem.

## Correct

✅ Discuss the problem.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Enter into the room.

## Correct

✅ Enter the room.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Married with her.

## Correct

✅ Married to her.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Good in English.

## Correct

✅ Good at English.

---

# 🟣 Word Order Errors

English follows a standard word order:

```text
Subject + Verb + Object
```

---

## Incorrect

❌ Always I wake up early.

## Correct

✅ I always wake up early.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Beautiful is this place.

## Correct

✅ This place is beautiful.

---

## Incorrect

❌ Never I have seen this.

## Correct

✅ I have never seen this.

---

# 🔴 Double Negative Errors

Using two negatives makes the sentence incorrect in standard English.

---

## Incorrect

❌ I don't know nothing.

## Correct

✅ I don't know anything.

---

## Incorrect

❌ He never said nothing.

## Correct

✅ He never said anything.

---

## Incorrect

❌ She didn't meet nobody.

## Correct

✅ She didn't meet anybody.

---

# 🟢 Frequently Confused Words

| Incorrect     | Correct                            |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Your welcome  | You're welcome                     |
| Its raining   | It's raining                       |
| Their going   | They're going                      |
| Loose weight  | Lose weight                        |
| Accept advice | Accept advice / Except = excluding |
| Affect (verb) | Effect (noun)                      |
