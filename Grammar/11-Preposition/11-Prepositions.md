# 📖 What is a Preposition?

A **Preposition** is a word that shows the **relationship** between a noun (or pronoun) and another word in the sentence.

It can show:

* 📍 Place
* ⏰ Time
* ➡️ Direction
* 🚶 Movement
* 🤝 Relationship

---

# 🌍 Examples

### Example 1

> The book is **on** the table.

**On** shows the relationship between **book** and **table**.

---

### Example 2

> She arrived **at** 9:00 AM.

**At** shows time.

---

### Example 3

> He walked **into** the room.

**Into** shows movement.

---

### Example 4

> They live **near** the station.

**Near** shows location.

---

# ⏰ 1. Prepositions of Time

These prepositions tell us **when** something happens.

### Common Time Prepositions

* At
* On
* In
* Before
* After
* Since
* For
* During
* Until
* By

---

# ⭐ Rule 1: Use **AT** for Exact Time

Use **at** for:

* Clock time
* Specific moments
* Festivals (in some expressions)

---

### Examples

✅ The class starts **at** 8:00 AM.

✅ I wake up **at** sunrise.

✅ Let's meet **at** midnight.

---

# ⭐ Rule 2: Use **ON** for Days & Dates

Use **on** with:

* Days
* Dates
* Special days

---

### Examples

✅ The exam is **on** Monday.

✅ Her birthday is **on** 15 August.

✅ We met **on** Christmas Day.

---

# ⭐ Rule 3: Use **IN** for Longer Time Periods

Use **in** with:

* Months
* Years
* Seasons
* Centuries
* Parts of the day

---

### Examples

✅ I was born **in** 2005.

✅ Flowers bloom **in** spring.

✅ We study **in** the morning.

---

# 📊 Quick Comparison

| Preposition | Used For     | Example   |
| ----------- | ------------ | --------- |
| At          | Exact time   | At 7 PM   |
| On          | Days & Dates | On Sunday |
| In          | Months/Years | In July   |

# 📍 2. Prepositions of Place

These tell us **where** something is.

---

## ⭐ Rule 1: AT (Specific Point)

Use **at** for a specific place.

### Examples

✅ She is **at** the bus stop.

✅ He is **at** school.

✅ We met **at** the airport.

---

## ⭐ Rule 2: IN (Inside Something)

Use **in** when something is enclosed.

### Examples

✅ The keys are **in** the bag.

✅ She lives **in** Delhi.

✅ Fish swim **in** water.

---

## ⭐ Rule 3: ON (Surface)

Use **on** when something is touching a surface.

### Examples

✅ The phone is **on** the table.

✅ There is a picture **on** the wall.

✅ The laptop is **on** the desk.

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

❌ I was born **at** 2005.

✅ I was born **in** 2005.

---

## Mistake 2

❌ The class starts **in** 8 AM.

✅ The class starts **at** 8 AM.

---

## Mistake 3

❌ The exam is **in** Monday.

✅ The exam is **on** Monday.

---

## Mistake 4

❌ The mobile is **in** the table.

✅ The mobile is **on** the table.
