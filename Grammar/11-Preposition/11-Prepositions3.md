# 📝 Practice Questions

Test your understanding of **Prepositions**.

---

## Question 1

She was born ___ 2005.

A. at
B. on
C. in
D. by

✅ **Answer:** **C. in**

**Explanation:** Years ke saath **in** use hota hai.

---

## Question 2

The meeting starts ___ 9:00 AM.

A. in
B. at
C. on
D. from

✅ **Answer:** **B. at**

---

## Question 3

The book is ___ the table.

A. in
B. on
C. into
D. over

✅ **Answer:** **B. on**

---

## Question 4

He walked ___ the room.

A. in
B. into

The train passed ___ the tunnel.

# 🎯 Placement Level MCQs

### Q1. Choose the correct sentence.

A. I was born at 2006.

B. I was born on 2006.

C. I was born in 2006.

D. I was born by 2006.

✅ **Answer:** **C**

### Q2. Which sentence is correct?

A. He jumped in the pool.

B. He jumped into the pool.

C. He jumped on the pool.

D. He jumped at the pool.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q3. Which preposition shows duration?

A. Since

B. During

C. For

D. By

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q4. Choose the correct option.

A. Sit besides me.

B. Sit beside me.

C. Sit between me.

D. Sit among me.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

### Q5. Which sentence is correct?

A. The cat is in the table.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

# 💼 Prepositions in Professional Communication

Correct prepositions make your English more natural and professional.

---

## Resume Example

❌ Incorrect:

> Experienced **at** Python.

✅ Correct:

> Experienced **in** Python.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I am interested **on** AI.

✅ Correct:

> I am interested **in** Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Workplace Example

❌ Incorrect:

> We discussed **about** the project.

✅ Correct:

> We discussed the project.

**Note:** The verb **discuss** does **not** take **about**.

---

## Email Example

❌ Incorrect:

> Please reply on this email.

✅ Correct:

> Please reply to this email.

# ⚠️ Common Errors

### Error 1

❌ Married with someone

✅ Married to someone

---

### Error 2

❌ Good in Mathematics

✅ Good at Mathematics

---

### Error 3

❌ Different than (formal British usage)

✅ Different from

---

### Error 4

❌ Depend in

✅ Depend on

---

### Error 5

❌ Listen music

✅ Listen to music

# 📄 Preposition Cheat Sheet

```text id="prepfinal"

TIME

At → Exact Time
On → Days / Dates
In → Months / Years


PLACE

At → Specific Point
On → Surface
In → Inside


DIRECTION

To → Destination
Into → Movement Inside
Onto → Movement to Surface
Towards → Direction


MOVEMENT

Across → One Side to Another
Through → Inside
Along → Beside
Around → Surrounding
Over → Above
Under → Below


COMMON PAIRS

Interested in
Good at
Depend on
Listen to
Married to
Different from

```
