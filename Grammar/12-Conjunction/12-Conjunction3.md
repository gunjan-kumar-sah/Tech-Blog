# 📝 Practice Questions

Test your understanding of **Conjunctions**.

---

## Question 1

Rahul is intelligent ___ hardworking.

A. but

B. or

C. and

D. because

✅ **Answer:** **C. and**

---

## Question 2

He studied hard, ___ he failed.

A. and

B. but

C. because

D. so

✅ **Answer:** **B. but**

---

## Question 3

Work hard ___ you will succeed.

A. and

B. but

C. because

D. nor

✅ **Answer:** **A. and**

---

## Question 4

She stayed home ___ she was sick.

A. although

B. because

C. but

D. or

✅ **Answer:** **B. because**

---

## Question 5

___ you study regularly, you will pass.

A. If

B. But

C. Yet

D. Nor

✅ **Answer:** **A. If**

---

## Question 6

Neither Rahul ___ Aman attended the meeting.

A. and

B. or

C. nor

D. but

✅ **Answer:** **C. nor**

---

## Question 7

She is ___ intelligent ___ hardworking.

A. either...or

B. neither...nor

C. both...and

D. because...so

✅ **Answer:** **C. both...and**

---

## Question 8

He was tired, ___ he completed the project.

A. because

B. yet

C. for

D. since

✅ **Answer:** **B. yet**

---

## Question 9

I will wait here ___ you return.

A. when

B. until

C. because

D. although

✅ **Answer:** **B. until**

---

## Question 10

Choose the correct pair:

A. Either...and

B. Neither...or

C. Both...and

D. Because...but

✅ **Answer:** **C. Both...and**

# 🎯 Placement Level MCQs

### Q1. Which sentence is correct?

A. Although but he tried, he failed.

B. Although he tried, he failed.

C. Because but he tried.

D. He tried although but failed.

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q2. Which conjunction shows **contrast**?

A. And

B. Or

C. But

D. So

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q3. Which conjunction shows **reason**?

A. Because

B. Yet

C. Or

D. Nor

✅ **Answer:** **A**

---

### Q4. Which conjunction pair is correct?

A. Neither...or

B. Either...and

C. Both...and

D. Because...so

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q5. Identify the subordinating conjunction.

A. And

B. But

C. If

D. Yet

✅ **Answer:** **C**

# 💼 Conjunctions in Professional Communication

Using conjunctions correctly improves the flow of your writing.

---

## Resume Example

❌ Incorrect:

> Skilled in Python but Java and AI.

✅ Correct:

> Skilled in Python, Java, and Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Interview Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I worked hard because so I wanted success.

✅ Correct:

> I worked hard because I wanted success.

OR

✅ I wanted success, so I worked hard.

---

## Workplace Example

❌ Incorrect:

> We completed the project but successfully.

✅ Correct:

> We completed the project successfully, and the client appreciated our work.

---

## Email Example

❌ Incorrect:

> I was busy because so I couldn't reply.

✅ Correct:

> I was busy, so I couldn't reply.
