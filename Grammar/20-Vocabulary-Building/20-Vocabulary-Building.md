# 📖 What is Vocabulary?

**Vocabulary** is the collection of words that a person understands and uses while speaking, writing, reading, and listening.

A strong vocabulary helps you:

- Communicate clearly
- Understand books and articles
- Write professional emails
- Perform better in interviews
- Score higher in competitive exams

---

# 🎯 Why Build Your Vocabulary?

A rich vocabulary improves:

- ✔ Speaking Skills
- ✔ Writing Skills
- ✔ Reading Speed
- ✔ Listening Ability
- ✔ Public Speaking
- ✔ Professional Communication
- ✔ Confidence

---

# 📚 Types of Vocabulary

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Reading Vocabulary | Words you understand while reading |
| Listening Vocabulary | Words you understand while listening |
| Speaking Vocabulary | Words you use while speaking |
| Writing Vocabulary | Words you use while writing |

# 🌱 Root Words

A **root word** is the basic form of a word. Many English words are formed by adding prefixes and suffixes to root words.

## Examples

| Root Word | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Act | Do | Action, Active, Actor |
| Form | Shape | Formation, Reform |
| Port | Carry | Transport, Portable |
| Dict | Speak | Dictionary, Predict |
| Struct | Build | Construction, Structure |

---

# ➕ Prefixes

A **prefix** is added at the **beginning** of a word to change its meaning.

## Common Prefixes

| Prefix | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Un- | Not | Unhappy |
| Re- | Again | Rewrite |
| Dis- | Opposite | Disagree |
| Mis- | Wrongly | Misunderstand |
| Pre- | Before | Preview |
| Auto- | Self | Autobiography |
| Inter- | Between | International |
| Super- | Above | Superhuman |

---

# ➖ Suffixes

A **suffix** is added at the **end** of a word.

## Common Suffixes

| Suffix | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| -er | Person | Teacher |
| -ness | State | Happiness |
| -ment | Result | Development |
| -ful | Full of | Helpful |
| -less | Without | Fearless |
| -tion | Action | Education |
| -able | Capable of | Readable |
| -ly | In a manner | Quickly |

---

# 🌳 Word Families

Words that come from the same root belong to the same **word family**.

| Base Word | Word Family |
|-----------|-------------|
| Educate | Education, Educator, Educational |
| Create | Creator, Creative, Creativity |
| Employ | Employee, Employer, Employment |
| Write | Writer, Rewrite, Writing |
| Manage | Manager, Management, Manageable |

# 🌍 Everyday Vocabulary Examples

### Word: Improve

Meaning:

To become or make better.

Sentence:

✅ Practice every day to improve your English.

---

### Word: Achieve

Meaning:

To successfully complete a goal.

Sentence:

✅ She achieved her dream of becoming an engineer.

---

### Word: Confident

Meaning:

Feeling sure about yourself.

Sentence:

✅ Speak confidently during interviews.

---

### Word: Efficient

Meaning:

Working well without wasting time or resources.

Sentence:

✅ Python helps developers build efficient programs.

---

### Word: Opportunity

Meaning:

A good chance to do something.

Sentence:

✅ This internship is a great opportunity to learn.

---

# 📋 Tips to Improve Vocabulary

### Tip 1

Read newspapers, books, and blogs daily.

---

### Tip 2

Learn 5–10 new words every day.

---

### Tip 3

Use new words in conversations and writing.

---

### Tip 4

Maintain a personal vocabulary notebook.

---

### Tip 5

Revise words regularly using flashcards or quizzes.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1

Memorizing words without understanding their meaning.

---

### Mistake 2

Learning words but never using them.

---

### Mistake 3

Ignoring pronunciation.

---

### Mistake 4

Confusing similar words.

Example:

❌ Accept ↔ Except

❌ Affect ↔ Effect

---

### Mistake 5

Trying to learn too many words in one day.
