# 📝 Vocabulary Practice Questions

Test your understanding of **Vocabulary Building**.

---

## Question 1

Choose the synonym of **Happy**.

A. Angry

B. Joyful

C. Weak

D. Slow

✅ **Answer:** **B. Joyful**

---

## Question 2

Choose the antonym of **Success**.

A. Victory

B. Progress

C. Failure

D. Achievement

✅ **Answer:** **C. Failure**

---

## Question 3

Choose the correct word.

Rahul is a very ______ student.

A. intelligent

B. intelligence

C. intelligently

D. intelligible

✅ **Answer:** **A. intelligent**

---

## Question 4

Choose the correct collocation.

A. Do a decision

B. Make a decision

C. Create a decision

D. Build a decision

✅ **Answer:** **B. Make a decision**

---

## Question 5

Choose the correct homophone.

I want to ______ a new laptop.

A. by

B. buy

C. bye

D. bee

✅ **Answer:** **B. buy**

---

## Question 6

Choose the correct sentence.

A. Their going home.

B. They're going home.

C. There going home.

D. Them going home.

✅ **Answer:** **B. They're going home.**

---

## Question 7

Choose the correct word.

The teacher gave me useful ______.

A. advise

B. advice

C. advising

D. advisor

✅ **Answer:** **B. advice**

---

## Question 8

Choose the antonym of **Ancient**.

A. Modern

B. Old

C. Historic

D. Traditional

✅ **Answer:** **A. Modern**

---

## Question 9

Choose the synonym of **Brave**.

A. Weak

B. Coward

C. Courageous

D. Afraid

✅ **Answer:** **C. Courageous**

---

## Question 10

Choose the correct sentence.

A. Please pay attention.

# 🎯 Placement-Level MCQs

### Q1. Which word means **to receive**?

A. Except

B. Accept

C. Affect

D. Effect

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q2. Which word is a noun?

A. Advise

B. Advice

C. Advising

D. Advisable

✅ **Answer:** **B**

---

### Q3. Which pair contains antonyms?

A. Happy – Joyful

B. Rich – Wealthy

C. Increase – Decrease

D. Smart – Intelligent

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q4. Which is the correct collocation?

A. Strong rain

B. Powerful coffee

C. Heavy rain

D. Deep decision

✅ **Answer:** **C**

---

### Q5. Which word means **capable of being trusted**?

A. Flexible

B. Reliable

C. Modern

D. Powerful

✅ **Answer:** **B**

# 💼 Vocabulary in Professional Communication

A strong vocabulary helps you communicate clearly in professional environments.

---

## Resume

❌ I know many programming languages.

✅ I am **proficient in** Python, Java, and SQL.

---

## Email

❌ Please send the details fast.

✅ Please send the details **at your earliest convenience**.

---

## Interview

❌ I am good in programming.

✅ I am **proficient in** programming.

OR

✅ I have a strong foundation in programming.

---

## LinkedIn

❌ I learned many things.

✅ I gained valuable knowledge and practical experience during my internship.

# 📄 Vocabulary Cheat Sheet

```text
SYNONYMS

Happy → Joyful

Big → Large

Small → Tiny

Fast → Quick

Smart → Intelligent

---------------------------------

ANTONYMS

Success → Failure

Rich → Poor

Increase → Decrease

Ancient → Modern

Strong → Weak

---------------------------------

COMMON COLLOCATIONS

Make a decision

Take a break

Pay attention

Heavy rain

Strong coffee

Deep sleep

---------------------------------

HOMOPHONES

Buy ↔ By

Two ↔ Too

Right ↔ Write

Sea ↔ See

Flower ↔ Flour

---------------------------------

CONFUSED WORDS

Accept → Receive

Except → Excluding

Advice → Noun

Advise → Verb

Affect → Verb

Effect → Noun

Lose → Not Win

Loose → Not Tight
```
