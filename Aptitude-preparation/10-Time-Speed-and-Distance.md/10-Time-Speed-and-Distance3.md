# 📄 Quick Revision Cheat Sheet

```text
Distance = Speed × Time

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Time = Distance ÷ Speed

Average Speed = Total Distance ÷ Total Time

Downstream = Boat + Stream

Upstream = Boat − Stream

Same Direction = Difference of Speeds

Opposite Direction = Sum of Speeds

km/h → m/s = ×5/18

m/s → km/h = ×18/5
```
