# 🤔 What is Ratio?

A **Ratio** is a comparison between two quantities of the same kind using division.

### Formula

**Ratio = First Quantity : Second Quantity**

### Example

If there are **12 boys** and **8 girls** in a class,

Ratio of Boys : Girls

= 12 : 8

Simplify by dividing both numbers by 4:

= **3 : 2**

---

# 📌 Properties of Ratio

* Ratio has no units.
* Both quantities should be of the same type.
* Ratios can always be simplified.
* Ratio can be written as **a:b** or **a/b**.

---

# 🤔 What is Proportion?

A **Proportion** states that **two ratios are equal**.

Example:

2 : 4 = 4 : 8

Both ratios equal **1/2**.

Therefore,

**2 : 4 :: 4 : 8**

---

# 📝 Formula of Proportion

If

**a : b = c : d**

Then

**a × d = b × c**

This is called the **cross multiplication rule**.

---

# 🔥 Solved Examples

### Example 1

Simplify **18 : 24**

HCF = 6

18 ÷ 6 = 3

24 ÷ 6 = 4

**Answer: 3 : 4**

---

### Example 2

If **x : 12 = 5 : 6**, find x.

Cross multiplication:

x × 6 = 12 × 5

6x = 60

x = **10**

---

### Example 3

The ratio of boys to girls is **5 : 3**.

If there are **40 boys**, how many girls are there?

5 → 40

1 → 8

3 → 24

**Answer: 24 girls**

---

# ⚡ Shortcut Tricks

* Always simplify the ratio first.
* Use HCF for quick simplification.
* Memorize common fraction-to-ratio conversions.
* In proportion questions, use cross multiplication directly.
