# 📖 Vision

Our vision is to make **Tech-Blog** a free, open-source, and beginner-friendly learning platform where students, developers, and placement aspirants can find high-quality technical content in one place.

---

# 🎯 Mission

* 📚 Create structured learning paths.
* 💻 Build practical programming guides.
* 🎓 Help students prepare for placements.
* 🌍 Support open-source learning.
* 🤝 Encourage community contributions.
